Injectables and Dermal Fillers

What are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers treat moderate to severe wrinkles, folds, and collagen loss that can occur as we age. Areas that can be enhanced include the lips, nasal labial folds, parenthesis around the mouth, chin crease, hollow cheeks, and more. We can help you determine which filler is best for you. For some clients, multiple dermal fillers are used at once to obtain the optimum look.

Dermal fillers are a great complementary procedure to Injectables. Dermal Fillers will tackle static wrinkles and enhance the volume of your skin. Injectables like Botox work on the dynamic wrinkles in your face, like between your eyebrows. Our team will help you choose the best treatment plan for you and your goals.

What are Wrinkle Relaxers?

Injectible wrinkle relaxers are one of the most effective methods of cosmetic enhancement with immediate results. Botox Cosmetic® or Dysport might be the perfect solution to restoring that youthful, energized look we all crave. Injectibles relax facial muscles, leading to smoother skin and disappearance of unsightly lines and wrinkles.

Whether you are trying to get rid of specific wrinkles, like crows feet or prevent deep wrinkles before they start, you should consider the professional applying your injection as much as the product itself.
